This activity guide complements material in Geometry with an Introduction to Cosmic Topology. This guide includes web-based computational tools (primarily Sage cells) for working with complex numbers and transformation of the extended complex plane, and Geogebra tools and labs for working with clines in the plane. The guide also provides several worksheets for student group work or independent study. This activity guide was authored in PreTeXt and, along with the textbook, is freely available at mphitchman.com.
